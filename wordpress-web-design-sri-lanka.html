WordPress web development methods in Sri Lanka:


When it comes to WordPress web design there are two ways to develop your website.

1. Design your website using premium WordPress theme
2. Develop a unique custom WordPress theme for your website

Design your website using premium WordPress theme


WordPress themes are destined to control the graphical interface and the functionality of the websites. WordPress have premium WordPress themes that looks super awesome. If your requirements are minimum and only need visitors to contact you or if you are looking for a WordPress website for your business with a small budget, then this is the right choice for you. This option is suitable for the small hotels , restaurants, small offices or beauty salons etc.

Develop a unique custom WordPress theme for your website


Does your business need something unique ?
Does your business need to highlight call to actions ?
Does your business need direct website visitors to take some actions ?

If the answer for above is yes, then you absolutely need a unique custom WordPress theme for your website. Comparatively with the premium WordPress theme web sites this option is costly.

This is because custom WordPress theme design requires both web designers and web developers. Designer design the template and then web developer code the design into a WordPress theme. This is the best option for the large scale business.
